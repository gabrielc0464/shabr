Info

 Time

The months we are available for best experience is from April-October. From those 7 months it is the best climatic conditions-which indicates a better and safter fly. The time is from early in the morning at 8:00am-4:00pm being the last flight of the day

 Weather

If there is rain,strong wind or thunderstorm then the flight will be cancelled due to weather conditions.

 Flight

The whole flight will take approximately 2-3 hours so particapants should be willing to spend 2-3 hours of their time during the flight. The flight is 20-60 miles away depending on how many miles they want to travel.
